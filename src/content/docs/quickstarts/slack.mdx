## Get your webhook URL

**Create a Slack app:**
1. Go to [Slack API](https://api.slack.com/apps?new_app=1) and create a new app **From Scratch**
2. Choose an **App Name** and select your workspace

![Create Slack app](_slack-assets/send-messages-to-slack/app.png)

**Enable incoming webhooks:**
1. Go to **Features â†’ Incoming Webhooks**
2. Toggle **Activate Incoming Webhooks**

![Enable incoming webhooks](_slack-assets/send-messages-to-slack/incoming-webhook.png)

**Create webhook:**
1. Click **Add New Webhook**
2. Select your target channel

**Add to Val Town:**
1. Copy the **Webhook URL** from Slack
2. In your val's sidebar: **Environment Variable** â†’ set **slackWebhookURL**

![Copy webhook URL](_slack-assets/send-messages-to-slack/env-var.png)

You will get a Slack message every time you click `Run` on the `main.tsx` file. 

![Message sent](_slack-assets/send-messages-to-slack/sample-message.png)
